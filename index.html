Uncompromising Quality

We source our tea leaves from small-scale Kenyan farmers, with whom we have conducted decades of meticulous cultivation and harvesting practices. Our careful selection process ensures only the freshest, most vibrant leaves reach our processing facility.

State-of-the-Art Processing

We employ the latest tea processing technologies, meticulously crafted to preserve the natural characteristics of the leaves. From withering, to rolling (for orthodox) or cutting, tearing and curling (for CTC) to fermentation and drying, each step is throughly controlled to yield the ultimate cup.

Expertise and Dedication

Our technical tea processing team is constituted by highly experienced tea masters that are passionate about, and highly skilled in the art and science of tea processing. Their dedication to meticulous craftsmanship and unwavering attention to detail is construed in every cup.

Sustainability Focus

We embrace sustainable and socially inclusive practices throughout our operations, by seamlessly supporting the families that constitute the community from the factory catchment – including and not limited to farmers, pickers, factory workers and local businesses. We preserve the environment by practicing organic tea farming and other agroecological practices adjacent to the tea.

Ethical Practices

We practice ethical trade as is demonstrated by the various certifications with Fair trade, Rainforest alliance, ISO 2020, KEBS etc.

Traceability

Each batch of tea is barcoded for traceability and samples of each batch are clearly marked and stored in the factory even after dispatch of the tea to the warehouse.
